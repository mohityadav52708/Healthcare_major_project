Stroke in India: A Major Health Crisis

Stroke is a leading cause of death and disability in India, with increasing incidence rates driven by lifestyle factors and rising cardiovascular diseases. Key Risk Factors: Hypertension, diabetes, smoking, and unhealthy diets significantly contribute to stroke risk. Health Impact: Strokes can lead to severe disabilities, impacting quality of life and placing a burden on healthcare systems. Awareness and education about stroke symptoms, along with preventive measures and timely medical intervention, are essential to reduce stroke incidence and improve outcomes for affected individuals.
